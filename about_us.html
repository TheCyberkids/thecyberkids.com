About The Cyberkids Limited

The Cyberkids Limited is formed by a group of enthusiastic professional members. We are divided into two divisions: Information Technology and Education.

Led by experienced managers, The Cyberkids Limited consists of professional and talented IT experts from business analysis, project planning, front end design to backend implementation and after sales service. We are committed to provide value added services to customers. Besides general front end and backend design and programming, we provide thorough project planning and business analysis to ensure the appropriate functions and features are delivered.

Our Education Division comprises experienced teachers who are full of love and patience. With our studies and knowledge on children, we design the course contents from student’s aspect. With the use of a wide variety of teaching tools to draw students' attention, we deliver best-fit teaching courses that allow students to learn in a friendly environment.
